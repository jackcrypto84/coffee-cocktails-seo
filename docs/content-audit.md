## Prioritized Improvement Roadmap

### P1: Move from rewrites into cluster-building
- Publish the missing coffee troubleshooting pieces around water, channeling, flat milk drinks, and medium-roast V60 recipes.
- Publish cocktail support pieces for spritz dryness, smoke control, and flavor-pairing logic so the recipe pages now have a deeper surrounding cluster.
- Add exact, tested specs to the rewritten cocktail pages once firsthand testing notes are available.

### P2: Deepen the best taxonomy hubs
- Use the strengthened espresso, pour-over, bittersweet, and coffee-beans hubs as the next content planning anchors.
- Expand citrus-forward and floral only as supporting content earns it, not because the route already exists.
- Keep the Guides page focused on routing user intent rather than turning it into another broad keyword bucket.

### P3: Refine commercial and editorial systems
- Add vetted affiliate recommendations only after firsthand testing notes are attached to product comparisons.
- Expand citations and source references on fact-sensitive pages with real primary-source links per article.
- Re-audit after the next 15 to 20 articles to catch duplication and weak cluster branches early.
