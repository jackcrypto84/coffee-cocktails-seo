# Publish-Ready Checklist

## Before removing placeholders
- Replace every `[VERIFY FACT]` with a verified claim or cut the claim.
- Replace every `[CITE SOURCE]` with a real citation or remove the unsupported statement.
- Replace every `[ADD FIRSTHAND TEST NOTE]` with a real observed note from brewing, tasting, batching, or review.
- Replace every `[ADD ORIGINAL PHOTO]` with an approved original image or remove the media callout.

## Editorial fit
- Confirm the article still matches the brief's search intent and differentiation angle.
- Confirm the title, H1, metadata, and article promise align.
- Confirm the article is more useful and more original than a generic SERP summary.

## SEO and monetization
- Add the planned internal links.
- Confirm any affiliate block solves a real bottleneck and does not crowd the article.
- Confirm FAQ, schema, and image choices match the visible content.

## Hero image requirements
- Content complete.
- Metadata complete.
- Internal links complete.
- Unique hero image prompt complete.
- Final hero image present in `public/images/coffee/[slug].jpg` or `public/images/cocktails/[slug].jpg`.
- Alt text complete.
- Caption complete.
- Visual status is `image-ready` or intentionally approved for a temporary fallback.

## Final trust check
- Remove any line that sounds confident but is not supported.
- Remove any filler sentence that could belong in almost any article.
- Make sure the article reads like it was shaped by actual taste, workflow, or editorial judgment.
