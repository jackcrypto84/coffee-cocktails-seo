# Article Draft User Prompt Template

Use the brief below as the only source of truth.

Required output expectations:
- Match the brief's primary keyword, search intent, reader persona, angle, title direction, and outline.
- Keep the editorial voice practical and specific.
- Preserve unresolved placeholders instead of fabricating certainty.
- Include the required placeholders exactly where human review or evidence is still needed.
- Avoid generic transitions and canned SEO filler.

Brief fields to inject:
- Primary keyword
- Search intent
- Reader persona
- Angle of differentiation
- Working title
- Meta title
- Meta description
- Recommended URL slug
- H1
- H2/H3 outline
- FAQ ideas
- Internal links to include
- External evidence needed
- Product or affiliate opportunities
- Originality notes
- Sections requiring human expertise or testing
- Image ideas
- CTA ideas
