# Coffee Drink Image Prompt Template

Use this template when generating a hero image for a coffee recipe or coffee-drink article.

Template:

```text
Photorealistic premium coffee photography of {title}. Show the final drink exactly as served: {drinkColor}, served in {glassware}, garnish {garnish}. Style cues: {styleTags}. Realistic lighting, realistic liquid texture, clean composition, visually appetizing, no surreal styling, no fake garnish, no inaccurate vessel.
```

Rules:
- Match the real recipe format exactly.
- If the drink is layered, show the layering clearly and realistically.
- If the brew is black coffee, avoid making it look like milk coffee.
- Show accurate serving vessel, brew setup, foam level, crema, and ice state.
- Keep props minimal and editorial, not cluttered.
- Prefer descriptive natural light unless the recipe clearly fits a moodier bar-style setup.
