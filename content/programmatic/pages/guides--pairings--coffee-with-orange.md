## FAQ
### Why do coffee and orange sometimes taste clean together and sometimes taste stale?
- The result depends on bitterness quality, sweetness, and whether the orange is acting as aroma or bulk acid.

### Is orange better with espresso or filter coffee?
- Both can work, but the expression changes. Espresso often gives more density, while filter coffee can feel brighter. [VERIFY FACT]

### Should I use juice or peel first?
- Usually peel first if you want a cleaner, more controlled pairing.
