## FAQ
### Do chocolatey beans have to be dark roast?
- No. Many medium roasts show cocoa more cleanly than darker roasts once bitterness stays under control. [VERIFY FACT]

### Are chocolatey beans best only for espresso?
- No. They often work well in espresso, milk drinks, and lower-agitation filter brews where sweetness stays intact.

### Why do some chocolate-note coffees taste woody instead of comforting?
- Usually the roast is too developed for the recipe, or the extraction pushes the finish past sweetness into dryness.
