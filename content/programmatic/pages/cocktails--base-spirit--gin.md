## FAQ
### What is the easiest gin cocktail family to start with?
- Usually citrus-led and highball-style drinks because they make balance easier to read.

### Do all gins work the same in original cocktails?
- No. Some are dry and structural, while others read softer or more floral and need more restraint.

### Why do some gin drinks taste perfumed instead of fresh?
- Usually too many aromatic ingredients are competing, or the drink lacks enough acid, bitterness, or dilution control.
