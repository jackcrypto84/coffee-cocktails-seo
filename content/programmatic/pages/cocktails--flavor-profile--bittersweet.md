## FAQ
### What makes a cocktail bittersweet instead of just bitter?
- Usually a drink has enough sweetness, dilution, and aroma to make the bitterness feel layered rather than punishing.

### Are bittersweet cocktails always aperitif drinks?
- No. They often overlap with aperitivo styles, but they can also show up in spirit-forward or coffee-accented builds.

### How do I make bitter drinks more balanced without making them sweet?
- Work on dilution, citrus lift, and the quality of the bitter component before adding more sugar.
